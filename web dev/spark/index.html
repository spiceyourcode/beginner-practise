About Spark & Build

Spark & Build is a free virtual learning program for youth to explore digital skills in Web Development, Graphic Design, or Arduino. Open to beginners and open hearts, this initiative aims to create tech opportunities for underserved learners. Our mission is to ignite curiosity and build futures by providing accessible tech education to youth who might otherwise not have these opportunities.

Nelly Mogere

Founder

Passionate about creating equal opportunities in tech education, Nelly founded Spark & Build to bridge the digital divide and empower the next generation of tech innovators.
